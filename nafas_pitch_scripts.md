# üéì Q&A PSYCHOLOGICAL TACTICS

## 1. The Pause Technique
After tough questions, pause 2 seconds before answering. Shows you're thinking, not deflecting. Builds credibility.

## 2. The Reframe
Turn negatives into positives:
- **Q:** "What if customers don't buy?"
- **A:** "Great question about go-to-market risk. Here's how we've de-risked with warm leads..."

## 3. The Bridge
Don't know something? Bridge to what you DO know:
- **Q:** "Exact churn rate projection?"
- **A:** "Pre-revenue, no empirical churn yet. Based on similar MENA SaaS like Telda's 15%, we project 20% conservatively. Here's our retention strategy..."

## 4. The Evidence Drop
Always end with proof:
- ‚ùå "We think customers will buy."
- ‚úÖ "Gabes Municipality verbally committed. Warm leads at GCT and SIAPE. Here are their names."

## 5. Confident Uncertainty
Admit unknowns confidently:
- ‚ùå "Uh, not sure."
- ‚úÖ "Great edge case I hadn't considered. My instinct is [X], but I'd research [Y] for a definitive answer. Can I follow up after the pitch?"
